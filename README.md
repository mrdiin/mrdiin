Full Stack Web and Mobile Developer
-----------------------------------

As the Co-founder, Managing Director, and CEO of Innovateium (Pty) Ltd, I lead a team of skilled developers and designers in building cutting-edge solutions for businesses. I specialize in mobile app development using the Flutter (with Dart) SDK for cross-platform deployment, as well as full-stack development with the MERN (MongoDB, Express.JS, React.JS, and Node.JS) JavaScript stack. 

My extensive experience with cloud technologies such as Amazon Web Services (EC2), Digital Ocean, and Docker, along with expertise in web servers such as Nginx, Firebase, Appwrite, and Supabase, enables me to develop scalable, high-performance applications. I create visually stunning and user-friendly interfaces with my skills in product development and graphic design, including proficiency in Figma, Canva, Diagrams.net, Adobe Illustrator, and Adobe Photoshop. I also bring creativity to motion design with Adobe After Effects, Adobe Premiere Pro, DaVinci Resolve, and 3D artistry with Blender. 

I am proud to be part of the team that has won the inaugural Absa Bank Botswana Hackathon for developing an SMME management system, as well as being part of the SmartBots team that won two of the Botswana Digital and Innovation Hub's hackathons for developing the Marriage Registration and Change of Surname Services System as well as the Youth Development Services System under the Ministry of Youth, Gender, Sport, and Culture.
